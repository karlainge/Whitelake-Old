With studying at Whitelake you might be looking for accommodations, so where else to look than the Whitelake campus. There is a total of 100 bedrooms per floor with a great view to the riverside, on the bottom floor there is a gym and a swimming pool for student to enjoy. There are total of 4 building which accommodate the students and currently plans to expand to having more accommodations in the moonlight area.
