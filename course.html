Introduction of the course

The Bsc (Hons) computing course is placed in the newly built technology building. This has been the feature for most of our computer courses in the recent years with a 95% pass ratio. With this course you will be able to learn new skills and enhance the current skills you already have which include Computer Technology, Software Designing, Website Development, Networking, Project Management and System Development. In the third year there is a chance to be placed in work placement either locally or abroad.

Entry Citeria

For acceptance of the 2016 year of the course you will need:

  • Five GCSEs equivalent to C or above which inclues Maths and English
  • 220 UCAS points from three A-Levels or equivalent
  • BTEC National Diploma/Extended at MMM
  • Acces to HE in QAA
  • International Baccalaureate: 30+
  • Foundation Year in Computing: Pass

    • Portfolio Requied: No

    Interview Requied: No

    English Language If English is not your first language, it is required to show a English level of IELTS 6.0 with 5.5 or equivalent
